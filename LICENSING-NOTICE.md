# Licensing Notice

**ose-primer is MIT-licensed across the entire repository.** See [LICENSE](./LICENSE).

## Why MIT

`ose-primer` is a repository template. MIT is the lowest-friction choice for templates: downstream cloners can relicense freely without encountering FSL, GPL, or other delayed-open-source constraints they did not choose.

## Policy shift from ose-public

An earlier fork (`ose-public`) used per-directory licensing with FSL-1.1-MIT for product apps and MIT for shared libraries. `ose-primer` drops that split in favor of uniform MIT because it ships no products — only reusable scaffolding. The FSL conversion clause (two-year automatic relicense to MIT) is replaced by immediate, uniform MIT.

## Scope

Every file tracked in this repository — apps, libs, specs, governance, docs, tooling, `.claude/`, `.opencode/` — is covered by the root [LICENSE](./LICENSE). No nested per-directory LICENSE files override it.
